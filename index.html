TOP STORIES

Kohli equals Ponting after wait of 1019 days

71 centuries for Kohli in all International cricket across formats - 27 in Tests, 43 in ODIs and this being his maiden three-figure score in T20Is. Only Sachin Tendulkar with hundred centuries in 782 innings has scored more in all International cricket than Kohli's 71. Kohli is tied on the second spot with Ricky Ponting who took 668 innings to score as many.

India send Goswami off with a historic 3-0 series triumph

The winning farewell that India were hoping to give Jhulan Goswami was under threat when the visitors were bowled out for 169 despite half-centuries from Smriti Mandhana and Deepti Sharma. But the bowlers, led by Renuka Thakur (4-29), powered India's comeback as they clinched a 16-run victory in a low-scoring thriller at the Lord's on Saturday (September 24) to seal a 3-0 series victory.

Clinical West Zone clinch Duleep Trophy

West Zone, on expected lines, completed a facile win over South Zone on the final day in Coimbatore to clinch the Duleep Trophy. Having put themselves on the driver's seat by stumps on the fourth day, West Zone completed the formalities by picking up the final four wickets on the fifth morning.

BCCI announces schedule for elections

The Board of Control for Cricket in India's (BCCI) election season has officially set in, with the electoral officer announcing the schedule. As per a seven-page notification distributed among the state associations on Saturday (September 24), the deadline for the state units to nominate their members was Saturday (September 24) and the nominations are to be filed on October 11 and 12. The elections, of course,will be held in Mumbai on October 18, the day of the BCCI Annual General Meeting (AGM).
